Every feature, at a glance

NewsBlur is the most feature-rich news reader. Here's everything it can do.

Free

Intelligence Training

Train NewsBlur to show you what you want and hide what you don't. Thumbs up an author, tag, or title keyword to highlight matching stories in green. Thumbs down to fade them away. The more you train, the smarter your feeds become. No algorithm decides for you — you're in control.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Archive

Full-Text Training

Go beyond titles and tags. Train on the actual content of stories, so you can filter based on what's being said, not just the metadata. Perfect for catching topics that span multiple authors and feeds.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Pro

Regex Classifiers

For power users who need precision. Write regular expressions that match against story titles, body text, and URLs. Build complex filters that catch exactly what you're looking for, from CVE numbers to stock ticker patterns to specific phrase combinations.

Available on Premium Pro Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Free & up

Real-Time Feed Fetching

Stories are pushed to you as they're published. Free users get real-time updates via PubSubHubbub. Premium users get feeds checked up to 5x more often. Pro users get every single feed in their account fetched every 5-15 minutes, making NewsBlur a monitoring-grade tool for professionals who need to be first to know.

Pro: every feed, every 5 minutes
Free

Web Feeds

Follow any website, even ones that don't publish an RSS feed. NewsBlur generates a custom feed for any web page by monitoring it for changes. Follow Twitter profiles, Reddit threads, GitHub repos, product pages, job boards — if it's on the web, you can follow it.

Premium

River of News

Read all feeds in a folder as one scrollable stream. No need to click into individual feeds — just open a folder and read everything in chronological order. Combined with training, your River of News surfaces the best stories from across all your subscriptions.

Premium

Full-Text Search

Search across all your subscriptions with Elasticsearch-powered full-text search. Find stories by keyword, phrase, or combination. Search within a single feed, a folder, or across everything. Save searches as feeds for ongoing monitoring.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Archive

Story Archive

Every story archived and searchable forever. When you subscribe to a new feed, NewsBlur back-fills its entire history so you have the complete archive from day one. Stories never expire. Your reading history becomes a searchable personal knowledge base.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Archive

Ask AI

Ask questions about any story and get answers powered by your choice of AI model: Claude, GPT, Gemini, or Grok. Get summaries, key points, or ask follow-up questions in a conversation. Even transcribe audio and video content and ask questions about it.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Archive

Daily Briefing

An AI-generated summary of your top stories, customized to your interests and delivered right in NewsBlur. Get curated sections: top stories, long reads, stories from infrequent sites, widely covered topics, and custom AI-powered sections you define. Choose your delivery schedule and summary style.

Archive

Story Clustering

When multiple feeds cover the same story, NewsBlur groups them together so you only see it once. Semantic duplicate detection uses fuzzy title matching to catch stories that are similar but not identical. Read the version you prefer and skip the rest.

Premium

Text View

Many RSS feeds only include a summary or the first paragraph. Text view extracts the full story from the original website and displays it right in NewsBlur, with clean formatting and no clutter. Read full articles without leaving your reader.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Free

Original Site View

Read stories on the original website, rendered inline within NewsBlur. See the full design, layout, and interactive elements of the original page without opening a new tab. Switch between Feed view, Text view, and Original view per-feed.

Free

Shared Stories & Blurblog

Share stories with your own comments on your public blurblog. Follow friends to see what they're reading and sharing. Every NewsBlur user gets their own blurblog — a public page of shared stories that anyone can follow via RSS or on the web. Premium users can make their blurblog private.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Premium

Saved Stories & Tags

Save stories for later and organize them with custom tags. Tags are searchable, so you can quickly find saved stories by topic. Each tag gets its own RSS feed, so you can pipe your curated collections into other tools or share them.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Free

Email Newsletters

Read your email newsletters where they belong: in a news reader. Forward newsletters to your unique NewsBlur email address and they appear as a feed. Train, tag, search, and organize newsletters just like any other feed. No more cluttered inbox.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Premium

Notifications

Get notified the moment a feed publishes a new story. Set notifications per-feed on web (browser push), email, iOS, or Android. Apply your training filters so you only get notified about stories that match your focus. Never miss an important update.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Free

Track Changes

See how a story evolves after it's first published. NewsBlur tracks revisions and shows you a diff of what changed, highlighted in green and red. Useful for following developing news stories, corrected articles, or evolving product announcements.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Free

Themes & Customization

Make NewsBlur yours. Choose from light, dark, and auto themes across web, iOS, and Android. Customize your reading experience with multiple font choices, five text sizes, adjustable line spacing, compact or comfortable density, and image preview styles. Every setting can be different per-feed, so each site looks exactly the way you want it.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Free

Keyboard Shortcuts

Navigate everything without touching the mouse. Open feeds, scroll through stories, mark as read, save, share, train, switch views — all from the keyboard. Press ? to see the full list. Power readers can fly through hundreds of stories in minutes.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Free

Multiple Layouts

Every site can have its own layout. Choose from Split, List, Grid, and Magazine views. Set different layouts per-feed to match the content — grid for image-heavy sites, list for high-volume feeds, split for long-form reading. Customize column count, card height, and content density.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Free

IFTTT & Zapier

Connect NewsBlur to hundreds of other services. Automatically save stories to Pocket, Instapaper, Evernote, or Notion. Post shared stories to Slack, Twitter, or email. Trigger custom workflows when stories match your training filters. The full NewsBlur API is also available for custom integrations.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Free

Third-Party Apps

Use NewsBlur with your favorite reading apps. Full sync support for Reeder, ReadKit, Unread, lire, Fiery Feeds, Newsify, and many more. Your read state, saved stories, and training all sync across every app. NewsBlur's open API powers dozens of third-party clients.

Free

Native iOS & Android Apps

First-class native apps built for each platform, not web wrappers. The iOS app supports offline reading, background refresh, widgets, and all the same training, search, and sharing features as the web. The Android app matches feature-for-feature. Both apps are open source and actively developed.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Free

Infrequent Site Stories

Never miss a post from sites that publish rarely. The Infrequent Site Stories view collects stories from feeds that only post once a week or less, making sure those gems don't get buried under high-volume feeds. It's like a curated digest of your quietest subscriptions.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Free

Feed Statistics

See detailed analytics for every feed: publish frequency, average stories per month, subscriber count, last fetch time, and historical charts. Know exactly how active a feed is and when it last published. Useful for deciding which feeds to keep and which to prune.

Read more on the NewsBlur Blog
Free

OPML Import & Export

Bring your feeds from any reader or take them with you when you leave. OPML import brings over all your subscriptions and folder structure in seconds. Export anytime to get a complete backup. No lock-in, ever.

Open Source

Self-Hosted

NewsBlur is fully open source. Run your own instance with a single Docker command. Complete data ownership, no lock-in. Customize it with Claude Code, contribute to development, or just run it on your own hardware. The entire codebase is on GitHub.
