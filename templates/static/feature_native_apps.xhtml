Native Apps

First-class native apps built for each platform, not web wrappers. Everything you love about NewsBlur, on every device.

NewsBlur on iOS with native interface and full feature set

How it works

  1. Download NewsBlur from the App Store or Google Play
  2. Sign in and your feeds, training, and saved stories sync instantly
  3. Read offline with automatic background refresh
  4. Train stories, search, share, and tag just like on the web
  5. Use widgets to see unread counts and stories at a glance

Are the apps free?

Yes, both the iOS and Android apps are free. Premium features in the apps match your subscription on the web.

Do the apps work offline?

Yes. Stories are cached locally and the iOS app uses background refresh to stay up to date even when you're not using it.

Can I use third-party apps instead?

Absolutely. NewsBlur's open API powers dozens of third-party clients including Reeder, ReadKit, Unread, and many more.

Are the apps open source?

Yes, both apps are open source and actively developed on GitHub.
