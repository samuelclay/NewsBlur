Saved Stories & Tags

Save stories for later and organize them with custom tags. Your personal clipping library, searchable and shareable.

Saved stories organized with custom tags

How it works

  1. Save any story with one click or a keyboard shortcut
  2. Add custom tags to organize saved stories by topic
  3. Browse saved stories by tag in the sidebar
  4. Search across all saved stories and tags
  5. Each tag gets its own RSS feed for sharing or piping into other tools

How many stories can I save?

There's no limit. Save as many stories as you want and they're kept forever.

Can I share tagged collections?

Yes. Every tag generates its own RSS feed, so you can subscribe to it from another reader, share the link, or pipe it into IFTTT or Zapier.

Are saved stories searchable?

Yes, saved stories and their tags are fully indexed by search.

What plan do I need?

Saving stories is available on all plans. Tags and tag RSS feeds require Premium ($36/year) or higher.
