Email Newsletters

Read your email newsletters in your news reader, not your inbox.

Newsletters appearing as feeds

How it works

  1. Go to Account Settings to find your unique NewsBlur email address
  2. Forward or subscribe newsletters to that address
  3. Each newsletter sender becomes its own feed
  4. Train, tag, search, and organize newsletters like any feed
  5. Your inbox stays clean

Do I get a unique email address?

Yes, every NewsBlur account has a unique email address for receiving newsletters.

Can I train on newsletter content?

Yes, all intelligence training features work on newsletters just like regular feeds.

Is this available on the free plan?

Yes, email newsletters are a free feature.
