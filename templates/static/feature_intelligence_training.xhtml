How it works

  1. Open the trainer for any feed
  2. Thumbs up authors, tags, or title keywords you like
  3. Thumbs down what you don't want to see
  4. Stories are highlighted green (like) or faded (dislike)
  5. Train across folders or globally on Archive

Is training available on the free plan?

Yes! Basic intelligence training on authors, tags, and title keywords is free. Full-text training on story content requires Premium Archive.

Can I undo training?

Absolutely. Open the trainer and remove any thumbs up or down to reset.

Does training work across all my feeds?

You can train per-feed, per-folder (Archive), or globally (Archive). Per-feed training is free.
