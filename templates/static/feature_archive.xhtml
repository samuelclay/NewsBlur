How it works

  1. Subscribe to Premium Archive
  2. Every new story is archived permanently
  3. When you subscribe to a new feed, its history is back-filled
  4. Stories never expire or disappear
  5. Search your entire archive with full-text search

How far back does the archive go?

When you subscribe to a feed, NewsBlur back-fills its entire available history. Going forward, every story is kept forever.

What happens if I downgrade from Archive?

Your archived stories remain accessible, but new stories will start following the standard retention policy.

Can I search the archive?

Yes, every archived story is fully searchable with Elasticsearch.

What plan do I need?

Story Archive requires Premium Archive ($99/year) or Premium Pro ($29/month).
