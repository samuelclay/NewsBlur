How it works

  1. Select any story and click "Ask AI"
  2. Choose your preferred AI model: Claude, GPT, Gemini, or Grok
  3. Ask questions, get summaries, or request key points
  4. Enable Daily Briefing for an AI-generated summary of your top stories
  5. Customize briefing sections and delivery schedule

Which AI models are available?

Claude (Anthropic), GPT (OpenAI), Gemini (Google), and Grok (xAI). You can switch between them at any time.

Does Ask AI work with audio and video?

Yes, it can transcribe audio and video content and then answer questions about it.

What is the Daily Briefing?

An AI-generated summary delivered to your NewsBlur account with sections for top stories, long reads, widely covered topics, and custom sections you define.

What plan do I need?

Ask AI and Daily Briefing require Premium Archive ($99/year).
