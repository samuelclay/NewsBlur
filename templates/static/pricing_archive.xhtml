Frequently Asked Questions

Does Archive include everything in Premium?

Yes, Premium Archive includes all Premium features plus the archive, AI, and advanced training features.

How far back does the archive go?

When you subscribe to a feed, NewsBlur back-fills its entire available history. Going forward, every story is kept forever.

What happens if I downgrade?

Your archived stories remain accessible, but new stories follow the standard retention policy.

Can I use Ask AI on any story?

Yes, Ask AI works on any story in your feeds, including archived stories.
