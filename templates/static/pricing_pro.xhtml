Frequently Asked Questions

Does Pro include everything in Archive?

Yes, Premium Pro includes all Premium and Archive features plus real-time fetching, regex, and priority support.

Why is Pro monthly instead of yearly?

Pro's real-time fetching infrastructure costs more to operate, so it's priced monthly. You're paying for dedicated, high-frequency polling of up to 10,000 feeds.

What does 5-15 minute fetching mean?

Every feed in your account is checked at minimum every 15 minutes, with most feeds checked every 5 minutes. This is independent of how often the feed actually publishes.

Can I downgrade from Pro?

Yes, you can switch to Premium Archive or Premium at any time.
